Pranay Mithilesh Pulipati

Principal architect

Pulipati Pranay Mithilesh's journey in architecture is a testament to his belief that every design is a product of a complex thought process. For him, architecture goes beyond aesthetics—it is fundamentally about user experience and the quality of spatial environments. This philosophy has shaped his career, influencing his approach to every project he undertakes.

Pranay began his academic journey at Dayananda Sagar Institutions in India, where he earned his Bachelor’s degree in Architecture. His thirst for knowledge and drive to push the boundaries of conventional architectural practices led him to the University of Liverpool in the UK, where he pursued a Master’s degree in Architecture. This advanced education not only expanded his perspective but also exposed him to diverse architectural styles and innovative design philosophies.

Among his notable recognitions is the FOAID - Festival of Architecture and Interior Designing 2025, where he earned honors in two categories—Conceptual Architecture and Finalist - Ongoing Interior Design. Additionally, he has been recognized by Begin Up as the Trusted and Promising Architecture & Interior Designer of the Year 2025, Telangana, and has received the Golden Door Award for Excellence in Consultancy within the Architecture & Interior Design Domain.

Currently, Pranay serves as the Chief Architect at EyeCatch Architects in India, where he continues to push creative boundaries and redefine spatial experiences.

Medha Pulipati

Principal Designer

A visionary interior architect, Medha Pulipati seamlessly blends aesthetics and functionality to create immersive spaces. Since joining EyeCatch in June 2023, she has led impactful design projects, transforming client visions into refined environments.

With a diploma and master’s degree from Florence Design Academy (FDA), Italy, Medha brings a global perspective to spatial storytelling. She specializes in concept development, space planning, and material selection, ensuring precision and creativity in execution. Her expertise in coordinating with designers, animators, architects, and contractors ensures cohesion and compliance with industry standards.

Beyond design, Medha is a mentor and innovator, fostering talent within her team. Her notable projects—including Perumal Silks, Amyra, Goyaz, Ladia, and Anubhava Sweets—reflect her ability to merge tradition with modern principles.

Proficient in AutoCAD, SketchUp, V-Ray, and Adobe Creative Suite, she leverages cutting-edge tools to bring designs to life. Through strategic planning, client collaboration, and meticulous attention to detail, she continues to elevate interior spaces with vision and precision.

Our Process

01

IDEATION

We start with a comprehensive open-ended template of queries to gain a deep understanding of the client's vision and expectations - laying the foundation for everything that takes to the initiation.

02

FORMATION

Transforming insights into a structured well analysed plan, we craft mood-boards, models, and meticulous designs to shape every detail of the very idea to take shape as a project.

03

TRANSFORMATION

Turning concepts into reality, we build from the ground up. As we specialize in end-to-end projects, this phase encompasses everything from structural design to final decor.

04

IMPROVISATION

Feedback and refinements are essential, presenting challenges that drive meaningful improvements throughout the process. To ensure a seamless experience, we actively listen to the client's critique, shaping and perfecting our approach.

05

DELIVER

The most awaited moment - handing over the keys to a fully realized space, ready for our clients to step in and make it their own.
