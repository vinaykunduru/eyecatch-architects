Smart Furniture

Vijayawada

  • Smart Furniture

    Vijayawada

    2025

    Client

    Santhosh G

    Typology

    Commercial

    Size

    5100 S.ft

    Status

    Completed

  • The store transforms a long, narrow space into an organic shopping environment with paper cut-out-inspired curved dividers. These elements create distinct zones for furniture display and enhance perception. A muted brown palette lets the furniture’s color and texture stand out, enriching the immersive experience.

    Inspired by paper cuttings, curved partitions with frayed edges add a sculptural touch. These flowing divisions create intimate, functional spaces that foster emotional connection.

  • The small shop space led to an innovative solution to the design to prevent monotony and provide a rich and visually stimulating interior. The design provides maximum main road visibility despite a secondary road entrance

  • The winding path and curving dividers create intimate areas, enhanced by a warm brown color scheme. The design balances comfort and readability, encouraging exploration and a captivating experience.

  • Inspired by Kirigami, the furniture store's display area transforms structural elements into sculptural forms. Walls peel and fold back, creating dynamic partitions that support adjacent spaces and enhance aesthetic appeal. This design defines spatial boundaries while inviting exploration, blending functionality and artistry.

  • The display zone features fluid partitions crafted from flexible plywood, echoing paper cuts and folds. Sculptural elements curve, forming parabolic contours that integrate with the floor's geometry. Subtle geometric embossing adds tactile depth, enhancing visual rhythm and creating an immersive experience.

  • Roman-inspired curves and concealed cove lighting create a refined backdrop for furniture. A monochromatic palette ensures focus remains on the displays, supported by elegant architectural details.

  • The layout accommodates 15 furniture pieces, two of which are in window displays, and has basic facilities. Parabolic curves provide clear wayfinding, linked spaces, and efficient space use.

  • Parabolic divisions break linear monotony, creating well-defined, dual-sided display zones. Curved modules maximize display, ensure smooth flow, and add rhythmic movement to the space.

    Swooping partitions serve as visual dividers and display supports, blending beauty and functionality. This two-in-one design adds depth and continuous storytelling to the space.

Aakruti - Clothing Store

Gachibowli, Hyderabad

  • Aakruti - Clothing Store

    Gachibowli, Hyderabad

    2025

    Client

    Murali

    Typology

    Commercial

    Size

    3650 S.ft

    Status

    Completed

  • In a bustling mall setting, the store’s decor stands out with a dramatic 'Aa' installation and a sleek, curved wall that commands attention. A strategically placed video wall amplifies visibility, using dynamic digital content to engage passersby and attract walk-in customers through visual storytelling.

  • The store delivers a cohesive and immersive spatial experience, guided by gracefully curved SS pipe racks and dynamic triangular lighting that animates the space. The sculptural 'Aa' entrance installation serves as a bold brand statement, while a soft, refined color palette fosters a sense of calm. Thoughtfully planned zoning enhances comfort, fluid movement, and engaging visual connections throughout the store.

    The zoned layout ensures smooth navigation and ample display space, with gondolas for added hanging and a dedicated service area offering easy, private access to trial rooms.

  • Stainless steel rack borders, embellished with sculpted motifs, offer a sturdy yet culturally evocative structure for showcasing garments. Positioned in front, display counters made from luxurious teak wood feature delicate silver inlays, echoing the elegance of traditional Indian silverware influenced by Bidri craftsmanship.

  • The modern wear section at Aakruti redefines retail display with fluid stainless steel pipes, shaping movement and zones without rigid enclosures. These sculptural forms extend seamlessly to display counters, ensuring a cohesive visual flow.

  • Aakruti’s rack display transforms the shopping experience with a rhythmic blend of stocked shelves and hanging sections, intuitively guiding customers through the space. Sculpted stainless steel pipes define these zones, providing both structure and seamless visual flow. This dynamic layout enhances product visibility while fostering an engaging and fluid retail journey.

  • This project masterfully intertwines narrative and design, with SS pipes shaped like flowing threads to form display racks that seamlessly blend function and form. These precisely bent pipes double as partitions, hanging systems, and wayfinding elements, guiding visitors through the space with fluid elegance. Anchoring the brand identity, the sculptural ‘Aa’ installation serves as a striking focal point, while triangular stretch lights introduce a sense of dynamism. A muted, refined color palette balances the energy, creating a harmonious environment that fuses contemporary structure with timeless cultural nuances for a truly distinctive spatial experience

Amyra Silver - Jewellery store

Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh

  • Amyra Silver - Jewellery store

    Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh

    2023

    Client

    K Tulasi Das

    Typology

    Commercial

    Size

    1700 S.ft

    Status

    Completed

  • Introducing a new era of fluidic interiors at amyra silver jewellery. Nestled in a serene, modern space, the store exudes fluid elegance and refined sophistication. Soft, flowing curves, neutral tones, and smooth textures enhance fine jewellery displays, offering a serene, gallery-like experience.

  • The Facade layered semicircular forms create depth, movement, and a dynamic appearance. Subtle shadows and dimensionality give the structure a sense of motion, softening its modern aesthetic with gentle, repetitive curves.

  • The site Situated on a corner with dual exposure and flyover visibility, The design balances aesthetics with functionality, keeping customer flow around a circular display and strategically placing key areas around it. Hidden storage and green corner oases maximize space while enhancing comfort and elegance.

    Step into a world of fluid elegance, where every thoughtfully crafted curve creates harmony, inviting exploration in a space that’s both sophisticated and inspiring.

  • The store transforms a rectangular layout into a dynamic space with a central circular presentation area, breaking away from linear norms. Sculptural, jewellery-inspired elements and elliptical racks evoke fluidity and reflect the brand’s identity.

  • The “Flow of Curve” concept draws from the serenity of silver jewellery, guiding visitors through sculpted racks and a central display via a serpentine path. Soft purples, beiges, and gentle curves create a calm, sophisticated, and immersive brand experience.

    Elliptical shelves rise seamlessly from curved walls, blending furniture with architecture and echoing the form of jewellery. Sculptural countertops and intentional details create a cohesive narrative of elegance and function.

  • The display counters are geometrized to replicate the geometry of diamonds, featuring layered surfaces that resemble facets.

  • The cash counter incorporates soft curves that blend organically with the interior's language, creating an exquisite balance of form and functionality.

  • To enhance both functionality and aesthetics, stretch lights are ingeniously installed into the ceiling, adding a more organic character to the design.
