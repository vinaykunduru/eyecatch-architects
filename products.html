Ladia - Lab Grown Diamonds

JUBILEE HILLS, PUNJAGUTTA

  • Ladia - Lab Grown Diamonds

    JUBILEE HILLS, PUNJAGUTTA

    2023

    Client

    Akhil

    Typology

    Commercial

    Size

    32 S.ft

    Status

    Completed

  • Situated in a luxury diamond boutique, the chandelier evokes purity and sophistication, creating an immediate sense of opulence. It makes a striking first impression, setting the tone for an engaging retail experience.

    The centerpiece of the boutique is a bespoke chandelier inspired by ocean waves and pearl-like purity, echoing the brilliance of cultured diamonds. Designed to resemble cascading droplets, it creates a dynamic, sculptural wave that reflects light and narrative. More than lighting, it embodies the brand’s essence—where craftsmanship meets poetic design.

  • The installation captures the serene rhythm of waves frozen mid-motion, with suspended droplets and curved brass wings guiding the eye in a fluid choreography. As a moving art form, the chandelier invites guests to pause and immerse themselves in an atmosphere where opulence meets tranquility. It beautifully echoes the brand’s essence of elegance and calm.

    Hand-blown from delicate Borosil Italian glass, each droplet captures the purity of diamonds and the fluidity of ocean waves, suspended in a graceful wave design. Framed by curved, subtly lit brass wings, the chandelier blends craftsmanship, material innovation, and storytelling in a refined centerpiece.

  • The design draws from the serene experience of the ocean’s edge, creating an immersive environment that mirrors the purity and fluidity of lab-grown diamonds. A calming blue palette slows the heart rate and enhances the diamonds' brilliance, resulting in a luxurious yet tranquil space that embodies peaceful refinement.

Ladia - Capsule

JUBILEE HILLS, PUNJAGUTTA

  • Ladia - Capsule

    JUBILEE HILLS, PUNJAGUTTA

    2023

    Client

    Akhil

    Typology

    Commercial

    Size

    32 S.ft

    Status

    Completed

  • The wall-mounted brass jewellery stands are a true testament to refined design and craftsmanship. Inspired by the sleek, modern silhouette of a capsule and meticulously shaped from cylindrical brass forms, these stands exude a sophisticated and contemporary charm. Their elegant construction and warm metallic finish not only draw the eye but also elevate the overall ambience of the display environment.

  • Purposefully designed to address and overcome spatial limitations, these wall-mounted fixtures serve as both functional solutions and artistic statements. By lifting the presentation of jewellery away from conventional countertop displays, they maximize available space while introducing a fresh, vertical dimension to the showcase.

  • These stands play a pivotal role in enhancing the allure of cultured diamonds. Their minimalist yet luxurious form acts as a perfect backdrop, allowing the inherent brilliance and craftsmanship of the jewellery to take center stage. The seamless integration of form, function, and material creates a striking visual harmony that enriches the customer experience, fostering a sense of exclusivity and emotional engagement.

    More than just display tools, these jewellery stands contribute to a curated retail narrative—one where design sophistication meets thoughtful practicality. They infuse the space with character, transforming it into an immersive environment that not only showcases products but also tells a compelling story of innovation, elegance, and intentionality.
