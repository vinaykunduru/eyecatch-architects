Careers

EyeCatch Architects is a distinguished firm in South India, renowned for its excellence and numerous accolades. Among its notable recognitions is the FOAID - Festival of Architecture and Interior Designing 2025, where it earned honors in two categories—Conceptual Architecture and Finalist - Ongoing Interior Design. Additionally, the firm has been recognized as the Trusted and Promising Architecture & Interior Design Firm of the Year 2025, Telangana, and has received the Golden Door Award for Excellence in Consultancy within the Architecture & Interior Design Domain.

Since 1995, EyeCatch Architects has believed in the power of visual storytelling—creating experiences that captivate audiences and leave a lasting impact. Our philosophy is rooted in innovation, creativity, and attention to detail, ensuring that every project we undertake exceeds expectations.
