Amyra Silver - Jewelry store

Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh

  • Amyra Silver - Jewelry store

    Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh

    2023

    Client

    K Tulasi Das

    Typology

    Commercial

    Size

    1700 S.ft

    Status

    Completed

  • The design of the store adopts a rectangular floor plan and converts it into a vibrant, visually stimulating space. Apart from the necessary areas such as the studio, office, pantry, washrooms, and storage, the remaining space is imaginatively redesigned to form a central circular presentation area. This design deviates from the conventional linear design, creating an atmosphere of flow and close-up. Elliptical racks elegantly protrude from the curved walls, echoing the fluidity of silver jewellery. The counters are jewellery shape-inspired, blending detailed intricacies with sculptural beauty. The result is a well-planned, emotionally charged retail space that reflects the brand's personality.

  • Contextual Reference

    Located on a corner property with exposure from two sides and a flyover, the store has a fa√ßade covered in PVC pipes and augmented by cove lighting, duplicating the soft curves within. Video walls integrated within also maximize the exposure of the brand, making it prominent.

  • Originality

    The concept, "Flow of Curve," incorporates earthy forms drawn from the serenity of silver jewelry. A serpentine curve directs guests through the area, fashioning sculpted racks around a circular middle display. The soft light purple and beige colour scheme provides balance between richness and calmness, developing a sophisticated, contemporary brand image. Gentle curves and ellipses replace abrupt corners, establishing an immersive, poetic environment.

  • Functionality

    The design achieves an ideal balance of aesthetics and functionality. A circular display is used as the anchor for customer flow, and functional areas such as the office, studio, and storage spaces are carefully placed around it. Additional storage is conveniently hidden in leftover spaces, and welcoming green oases in corners by the entry provide comfort and elegance, utilizing space to the fullest without compromising on design.

  • Creativity

    The display counters are geometrized to replicate the diamonds' geometry with layered surfaces, which appear like facets. In order to lift functionality as well as aesthetics, stretch lights are ingeniously installed into the ceiling, adding more organic character to the design. The cash counter has soft curves that blend organically with the interior's language, creating an exquisite balance of form and functionality.

  • Innovation

    Shelves glide smoothly up from the curved walls, mirroring the lines of jewellery. These elliptical shelves blur the boundaries between furniture and architecture. Sculptural countertops abstractly depict jewellery forms, introducing a sense of continuity and narrative. Each detail is intentionally crafted, combining aesthetics with functionality.

  • Sensorial & Spatial Experience

    The warm, sweeping design encourages exploration, with gentle curves, textured variety, and warm glows of light creating a comforting environment. Displays are bordered in a manner that invites moments of discovery, and the inclusion of greenery and the subtle scent of wood further adds to a carefully crafted, calming atmosphere.

Ladia - Lab Grown Diamonds

JUBILEE HILLS, PUNJAGUTTA

  • Ladia - Lab Grown Diamonds

    JUBILEE HILLS,PUNJAGUTTA

    2024

    Client

    General Realestate

    Typology

    General Realestate

    Size

    1700 S.ft

    Status

    Completed

  • The interior of this lab-grown diamond boutique rediscovers the customer experience through the synthesis of luxury and contemporary. Taking cue from oceanic pearls that evoke the scarcity and beauty of diamonds, the atmosphere is enveloped in calming blues that ooze sophistication. The sculptural focal point, in the form of a beautiful chandelier designed as a wave with water droplet motifs falling over each other, reinforces the theme of the element. Subtle brass accents add a touch of opulence, creating a beautiful contrast. What were once structural columns that posed a challenge have been cleverly transformed into curved display racks that wrap around the pillars, enhancing the flow and showcasing the products beautifully. Even with a compact layout, the design is optimized for easy circulation and visual openness. This method elegantly merges artistic narrative with functional brilliance, taking the brand identity and customer experience to a higher level.

  • Originality

    This look is a new interpretation of luxury retail, taken from ocean pearl-inspired design elements that capture the uniqueness and beauty of lab-grown diamonds. The wave-formed chandelier featuring droplet forms is a great anchor for the story. The curved display fixtures that incorporate structural pillars further facilitate the flow in space, with brass touches and backlit onyx marble that add sophistication. This balanced composition of form and material not only enriches the retail experience but also emotionally and visually connects.

  • Innovation

    By converting structural columns into fashionable design features, the layered wave-shaped display racks introduce fluidity and depth, creating product presentation that is truly exceptional. The cash counter seamlessly integrates into the overall design by curving around adjacent pillars, harmoniously combining functionality with beauty. These innovative solutions confront spatial issues head-on, bestowing the space with a distinctive character.

    Contextual Reference

    Located in an urban context with metro-train pillars to block the path, the store's facade displays eye-catching co-lights in bold configurations to maximize visibility and build a bold identity amidst visual noise. This smart lighting guarantees the brand makes a statement, even amidst all the distractions surrounding it.

  • Sensorial and Spatial Experience

    The store offers a seamless customer experience, with curved racks that lead customers smoothly through the space. The onyx marble with back lighting provides a deep tactile sense, making the atmosphere welcoming. Light, texture, and form together create an experience that is not only memorable but also emotionally evocative.

  • Creativity

    The wall-mounted jewellery-stand, designed as an elegant capsule sculpted from a cylindrical form, stands out with its distinctive charm.

    Functionality

    A central seating area, sculpted in an organic form, enhances the space's appeal, while the cash counter, strategically placed on the left, ensures optimal visibility.
