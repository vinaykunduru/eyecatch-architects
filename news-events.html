EyeCatch Architects Wins Second Place at FOAID 2024 – A Proud Moment for Telugu Architecture

News,
Thursday February 6, 2025

EyeCatch Architects has secured second place in the prestigious Festival of Architecture and Interior Designing (FOAID) 2024, a nationwide event held annually in Delhi and Mumbai. Our firm was recognized in the highly competitive Conceptual Architecture category, with Chief Architect Pranay Mithilesh Pulipati receiving the award during the Delhi ceremony. This achievement marks a major milestone for EyeCatch Architects and brings pride to the Telugu architectural community. We are thrilled to share that our recognition was also featured on TV9, Telangana and Andhra Pradesh’s leading news channel. We extend our heartfelt thanks to TV9 for showcasing our success.
