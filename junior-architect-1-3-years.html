Junior Architect (1-3 Years of Experience)

EyeCatch Architects is a dynamic collective of 50+ architects, engineers, inventors, designers, researchers, video editors, and consultants from Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, and Karnataka, all working together from our corporate office in Hyderabad, Telangana.

Our portfolio spans innovative architecture, immersive design, and groundbreaking research, shaping environments that inspire and endure. From transforming urban landscapes to redefining interior experiences, our projects merge creativity with technical precision, ensuring lasting impact.

At EyeCatch, we believe in visual storytelling, crafting spaces that captivate and engage. Our multidisciplinary team thrives on collaboration, experimentation, and innovation, bringing fresh perspectives to every project.

While our expertise is driven by visionary leadership, we maintain a flat hierarchy to foster creativity, allowing diverse minds to contribute meaningfully. Our knowledge-sharing initiatives include workshops, study programs, and industry collaborations, ensuring continuous growth and evolution.

With a deep commitment to excellence and forward-thinking solutions, EyeCatch Architects is shaping the future—one bold, transformative design at a time.

Join Us on Our Mission

If you are a Junior Architect, we’re looking for someone who has:

  • Completed an accredited bachelor's or master's degree in architecture or design.
  • 1+ years of postgraduate experience in architecture or a related field.
  • Willingness to relocate to Hyderabad or Vijayawada.
  • Proficiency in AutoCAD, SketchUp, V-Ray, Enscape, and Adobe Creative Cloud (Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign).
  • Strong conceptual and design skills.
  • Ability to develop design options in collaboration with design teams and take initiative in response to project leadership.
  • Ability to develop and edit drawings and models related to projects.
  • Strong organizational skills, ability to work under pressure, meet deadlines, and manage time efficiently.
  • A flexible and open attitude towards new and iterative ways of working.
  • Excellent communication skills, with fluency in English, Hindi, and Telugu (spoken).
  • Well-rounded project experience in Concept Design and Detailed Design is preferred.

At EyeCatch Architects, We Value:

  • Proactive Mindset – Take initiative, explore new ideas, conduct research, and aim to prevent problems before they arise.
  • Dependability – Be someone leadership can count on to follow through and deliver results.
  • Accountability – Own your decisions and actions, whether in daily tasks or long-term goals.
  • Consistency – Demonstrate reliability by delivering quality work time and again.
  • Tenacity – Stay committed, show up with enthusiasm, contribute your best, and persevere through challenges.
  • Independence – Take charge of tasks and projects confidently while knowing when to seek guidance.
  • Intelligence & Curiosity – Think critically, stay informed, be resourceful, and always keep the bigger picture in mind.
  • Generosity – Share your knowledge, support your teammates, and take time to mentor when possible.
  • Kindness – Be respectful, collaborative, and approachable. Foster a positive work culture and enjoy the journey together.
